STEM Engineer (Project Design & Classroom Support)

Location: Bekaa Valley, Lebanon

Job Type: Full-Time

Salary: Based on experience – applicants should state their salary expectations

Start Date: Immediate

About Us

CedarLabs is an education center dedicated to providing engaging STEM learning experiences through hands-on projects, engineering, coding, and design.

Job Description

We are seeking a highly motivated STEM Engineer to design new projects, assist teachers, and support classroom activities. Candidates should have expertise in coding, 3D printing, Fusion 360, and circuit design.

Responsibilities

  • Design and develop engaging STEM projects for students.
  • Support teachers with technical expertise and troubleshooting.
  • Assist in classrooms with hands-on activities.
  • Use 3D printing, CNC machines, and laser cutting for project components.
  • Develop CAD designs using Fusion 360.
  • Design and prototype electronic circuits.
  • Teach students coding, robotics, and circuit design.
  • Maintain and ensure proper functionality of lab equipment.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics, or related STEM field.
  • Proficiency in Python, Arduino, and coding-related tools.
  • Experience with 3D printing and Fusion 360 for CAD design.
  • Familiarity with circuit design and PCB development.
  • Passion for STEM education and working with students.

Why Join Us?

  • Be part of a growing education center focused on innovation.
  • Design and implement cutting-edge educational projects.
  • Work in a collaborative and creative environment.
  • Help students develop technical and engineering skills.

How to Apply

Send your CV, cover letter, salary expectations, and a portfolio of projects to jobs@cedarlb.com with the subject line "STEM Engineer Application - [Your Name]".
