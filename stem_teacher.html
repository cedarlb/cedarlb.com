Teacher (Structured Learning & STEM – Open to New Graduates)

Location: Bekaa Valley, Lebanon

Job Type: Full-Time

Salary: Based on experience – applicants should state their salary expectations

Start Date: Immediate

About Us

CedarLabs is an education center dedicated to providing engaging and interactive learning experiences through structured learning programs in math, literacy, and hands-on STEM activities.

Job Description

We are looking for a motivated teacher to join our team. Candidates should have a background in structured learning and a passion for education. Experience in STEM subjects is a plus, but not required.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and deliver engaging lessons tailored to different age groups.
  • Use a structured, step-by-step approach to teach math and literacy.
  • Create an interactive classroom environment that encourages learning.
  • Encourage problem-solving, critical thinking, and independent learning.
  • Develop hands-on activities and STEM-related projects.
  • Monitor and assess student progress, providing feedback.
  • Maintain a well-organized and safe learning environment.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Education (BSc in Education required).
  • Experience teaching children preferred; new graduates encouraged to apply.
  • Familiarity with structured, self-paced learning approaches.
  • Strong classroom management and communication skills.
  • Fluency in English and Arabic.
  • Interest in STEM education is a plus but not required.

Why Join Us?

  • Be part of an innovative and growing education center.
  • Great opportunity for new graduates to gain hands-on teaching experience.
  • Supportive environment for curriculum development and skill-building.
  • Opportunity to teach both structured learning and interactive STEM lessons.

How to Apply

Send your CV, cover letter, and salary expectations to jobs@cedarlb.com with the subject line "Teacher Application - [Your Name]".
